Steps to Make a Simple Business Card

Assembly time: 30-45 minutes
Difficulty level: 3/10

Are you wondering how to make a business card in Photoshop? Making your own card is easier than it looks. With a few tools and a clear layout, you can create a clean and professional card that shows your name, job, and contact information. This guide will walk you through the basic steps so you can design and save your card for printing.

  • Things You'll Need

  • A Computer With Photoshop
  • Your Business Information
  • A Logo or Icon if You Have One
  • A Color or Design Idea for the Card
  • A Printer
  • Card Stock

  1. Create a new document.

    Open Photoshop and create a new file. Set the size to 3.5 by 2 inches and the resolution to 300 DPI so it prints clearly. Add a small bleed if your printer needs it..

  2. Choose a background.

    Pick a simple color, gradient, or light pattern. Make sure it does not make the text hard to read..

    Colored Business Card Completed.

  3. Add Your Name and Job Title

    Select the Text Tool and type your name and job title. Make the font easy to read and place it near the top or center of the card.

  4. Add Your Contact Information.

    Type in your phone number, email, website, or social media handles. Arrange them neatly under your name so the card looks organized.

  5. Insert a Logo or Icon.

    Add any logos or icons you want on the card. Resize them so they fit nicely without crowding the text.
